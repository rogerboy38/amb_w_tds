Root Cause Analysis
The Core Problem: Partial SLE Creation During Stock Entry Submission
The stock entry submission in ERPNext creates Stock Ledger Entries in a transaction. When the transaction fails partway through, you get:

MAT-STE-2026-00330 (Material Transfer): The submission completed far enough to create the FG debit SLEs but the WIP credit SLEs were never written. The entry shows as "Submitted" (docstatus=1) even though it's functionally incomplete.

MAT-STE-2026-00336 (Manufacture): A partial SLE was created for the WIP debit of item 0334 — but it's stuck in a to_rename=1 state with zero valuation, and the corresponding LBL0334 WIP debit and 0334 FG credit SLEs were never created.

Why the Bin Shows -1800 in WIP
WIP never received the +1800 from the material transfer (missing SLE), but did get the -1800 debit from the manufacture entry (corrupt SLE). Net: 0 - 1800 = -1800. The ERPNext Bin table gets updated directly by SLE creation, so even the corrupt c0097fa632 SLE caused the bin to decrement.

Why FG Shows 0 Actual / 1800 Reserved
Actual = 0: The FG receipt SLE for the finished item (1800 Kg of 0334) was never created by MAT-STE-00336.

Reserved = 1800: The source of this reserved qty is unclear — there are no Sales Orders, Delivery Notes, or Stock Reservation Entries for item 0334 in the system. This may be a stale/phantom value in the Bin record that resulted from the failed submission attempts interacting with ERPNext's internal reservation mechanism during WO processing.
