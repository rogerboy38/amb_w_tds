Our products
============


Discover the best aloe vera that AMB has for you!




[![]()![]()](https://amb-wellness.com/aloe-vera-juice)
#### [Innovaloe Aloe Vera Juice](https://amb-wellness.com/aloe-vera-juice)

The Aloe vera plant has been known and used for centuries for its health, beauty, medicinal, and skincare properties. Aloe vera contains 75 potentially active constituents: vitamins, enzymes, minerals, sugars, lignin, saponins, salicylic acids, and amino acids. This Premium Aloe Vera Gel Juice is made from selected three years old of maturity Aloe Vera Leaves, rich in Polysaccharides and all the nutrients and bioactive components of this miracle plant.





[![]()![]()](https://amb-wellness.com/concentrates)
#### [Innovaloe Aloe Vera Concentrate](https://amb-wellness.com/concentrates)

Aloe Vera Gel Concentrate (NLT 5% Solids of Aloe); Premium Aloe Vera Gel Concentrate from AMB Wellness, made with selected Aloe Vera Leaves with 3 years old of maturity, rich in Polysaccharides and all the nutrients & bioactive components of this miracle plant.





[![]()![]()](https://amb-wellness.com/spray-dried-powder)
#### [Innovaloe Aloe Vera Spray Dried Powder](https://amb-wellness.com/spray-dried-powder)

Aloe Vera Gel Spray Dried Powder 200:1 (100% Solids of Aloe); Aloe Vera Gel Spray Dried Powder from AMB Wellness, made with selected Aloe Vera Leaves with 3 years old of maturity, rich in Polysaccharides and all the nutrients & bioactive components of this miracle plant.






[![]()![]()](https://amb-wellness.com/acetypol)
#### [Acetypol](https://amb-wellness.com/acetypol)

ACETYPOL™ is perfect for formulations intended for high-end Cosmetics, health care, and nutraceuticals for its high efficacy. The nutraceutical and functional food industries endeavors to develop novel delivery systems, which require excipients that fulfill specific functions. The unique properties of ACETYPOL™ will improve your marketing value, imparting, and giving a benefit in any product application.





[![]()![]()](https://amb-wellness.com/highpol)
#### [Highpol](https://amb-wellness.com/highpol)

Highpol™ is made through an exclusive technique from the isolation of polysaccharides in the Inner Gel of Aloe Vera. This micro filtering process reaches up to 40% of Total Polysaccharides measured by HPLC/GPC. Polysaccharides, more commonly called glycosaminoglycans, are a unique form of polysaccharides. They are made in the human body and perform many vital functions in our health, including promoting growth and enhancing the immune system.
